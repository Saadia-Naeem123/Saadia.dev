About

Building AI systems that reason, not just respond

I'm Saadia Naeem, an Artificial Intelligence undergraduate based in Haripur, Pakistan, with hands-on experience designing agentic AI systems using LangChain, LangGraph, and the Model Context Protocol (MCP).

My work spans autonomous agents with memory, planning, and tool-calling; computer vision products like virtual try-on and face-shape detection; and backend systems built with Django, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. I've also shipped decentralized applications on Ethereum.

I enjoy taking AI systems from a research idea to a production-ready product — the kind recruiters and engineering teams can actually put in front of users.
